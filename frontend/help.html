Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take? ›
In Anakapalli and nearby areas, most orders arrive in 15–35 minutes depending on your location and time of day. Peak hours (lunch/dinner) may take slightly longer.
What is your delivery radius? ›
We currently serve within 25 km radius of Anakapalli town center. Some villages may have limited availability — please check your location during checkout.
Can I cancel or change my order? ›
Yes — you can cancel or modify your order within 3 minutes of placing it. After that, please contact support immediately via call or WhatsApp.
What payment methods do you accept? ›
We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Netbanking, Wallets (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm), and Cash on Delivery (limited areas).
Do you offer refunds? ›
Yes — full refund for wrong/missing/damaged items. Please report within 30 minutes of delivery. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.
How do I track my order? ›
You’ll receive real-time updates in the app under "My Orders". You can also track live location of your delivery partner.
